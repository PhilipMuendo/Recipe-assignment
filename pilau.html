Delicious Pilau Recipe

Try this tasty pilau recipe for a taste of tradition.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Chicken or Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Water
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Add onions, garlic, and ginger in a pan.
  3. Add your choice of meat and brown it.
  4. Add tomatoes, cloves and cinnamon
  5. Stir in the rice and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Pour in water and bring to a boil.
  7. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes or until rice is tender.

Enjoy your Pilau!

This aromatic pilau recipe is a traditional favorite. Serve it with mango juice and enjoy!
